Minesweeper

I programmed a game of minesweeper in Javascript. But to add a small twist, I made it so that it infinitly generates. As you get close to an edge, it generates more tiles in chunks to offer a never ending experience. Simply click on a tile to flag it, or click on a number to clear out any spaces around that aren't flagged. Clicking with the scroll wheel will remove a tile.
